Lasagna

Description

A dish made with several layers of lasagna sheet noodles with sauce and other ingredients, such as meat and cheese.

This is a simple recipe. Please don't try it. It is for demonstration purposes only.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Braise the ground beef.
  3. Lay lasagna noodles onto pan.
  4. Spread sauce onto first layer of noodles.
  5. Add a layer of ground beef.
  6. Add cheese as necessary.
  7. Repeat step 1 for each layer, until pan is full.
  8. Bake in oven at 350 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes.
